Nosotros

Hailing from Sant Andreu, Barcelona, this simracing team embodies a dynamic duo of skilled Catalan pilots who light up virtual tracks with their prowess and unwavering passion for competition. Rooted in a competitive spirit that resonates with their Catalan heritage, these pilots seamlessly blend technical finesse with unwavering determination to achieve nothing short of excellence in every race. Beyond the confines of the track, their special camaraderie reflects a deeply ingrained pride in representing their homeland on the global stage of simracing.

From the winding streets of Sant Andreu to the most formidable virtual circuits, this team showcases their mettle through meticulously crafted strategies, lightning-fast reflexes, and unmatched competitive drive. The fusion of their love for motorsport and the rich Catalan culture manifests in every turn, creating a harmonious synergy that propels them towards triumphant victories.
